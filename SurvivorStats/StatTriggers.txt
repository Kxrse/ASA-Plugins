SurvivorStats - Stat Triggers
==============================

survivor_level - Your character levels up
survivor_kills - You kill another player
survivor_kills_mounted - You kill another player while riding a dino
survivor_kills_victim_mounted - You kill another player who is riding a dino
deaths_by_survivor - You are killed by another player
deaths_by_survivor_mounted - You are killed by a player who is riding a dino
deaths_while_mounted - You are killed by a player while you are riding a dino
wild_dino_kills - You kill a wild dino
wild_dino_kills_mounted - You kill a wild dino while riding a dino
tamed_dino_kills - You kill a tamed dino
tamed_dino_kills_mounted - You kill a tamed dino while riding a dino
deaths_by_wild_dino - You are killed by a wild dino
deaths_by_wild_dino_while_mounted - You are killed by a wild dino while riding a dino
deaths_by_tamed_dino - You are killed by a tamed dino with no rider
